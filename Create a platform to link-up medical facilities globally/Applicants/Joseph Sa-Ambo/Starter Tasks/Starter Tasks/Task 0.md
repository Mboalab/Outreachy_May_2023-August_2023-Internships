## Introduction
In this UX research project, I aimed to gather information on 50 hospitals around the world and collect their information including their location, services provided, and website links. The research goal was to understand the range of medical services offered by hospitals worldwide and to identify any trends or patterns in their services and online presence. These findings will help us create a suitable platform that links medical facilities globally and make it easier for people to access the medical services they need.


## Research Goals
The research goals for this project were:
* To identify 50 active global hospitals and their locations
* To gather information on the medical services offered by these hospitals
* To determine the online presence of these hospitals by gathering website links


## Methodology
To achieve these goals, I conducted a quantitative research exercise, by performing the following tasks:

* Online search: I searched for information on hospitals worldwide using online search engines and directories and I gathered the data in an organized form using Open AI‚Äôs ChatGPT.  I used keywords such as "active global hospitals" and "top hospitals worldwide" to find relevant information.

* Data collection: I gathered data on the hospitals' locations, services provided, and website links using Open AI‚Äôs ChatGPT, while cross-checking from each hospital‚Äôs website to confirm accuracy of information. I then recorded this data in a spreadsheet table for easy organization and analysis.

* Analysis: I analyzed the data to identify patterns and trends in the medical services offered by hospitals and their online presence.
